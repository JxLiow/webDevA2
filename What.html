What is Dragonboat





Dragonboat is a racing sport
that comprises from 12/22 people
on the boat

Next









Races can range from 200 metres
to 2000 metres (2 kilometres)

Previous Next


Like most team sports, Dragonboat requires everyone to work together. But this is harder for Dragonboating.

This is because dragonboating requires everyone to go at the same pace, so as to ensure smooth sailing.

Previous Next


There are 3 roles on the boat.

Steers

In charge of changing the direction.


Most important, as it holds the most control over the boat.

Drummers

Helps create a tempo for everyone to follow,
giving the whole boat a rhythm to follow.

Also in charge of increasing or reducing the boat's pace.

Paddlers

Most common role, in charge
of making the boat move.

Every paddler has to be in sync with each other
in order to ensure the boat glides with ease.
